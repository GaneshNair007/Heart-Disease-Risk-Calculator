# Heart Disease Risk Calculator 

A Python-based command-line tool that assesses a patient's risk of heart disease based on various health parameters.

##  Features
* **Patient Profiling:** Collects age, gender, and personal details.
* **Health Analysis:** Analyzes Blood Pressure, Cholesterol, Glucose levels, and Heart Rate.
* **Lifestyle Factors:** Accounts for smoking and exercise habits.
* **Predictive Logic:** Generates a risk score (0-100) and predicts potential symptoms.
